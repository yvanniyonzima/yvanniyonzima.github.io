Animal Care Shelter |

C++

System Development / January 2019 - April 2019

This project was a 4 month academic project. We were tasked as a group to design and implement an Animal Care Shelter System that keeps records of clients and animals awaiting for adoption and macth them based on their personality and the clients preferences in physiscal features. My role as the object model and algorithm desing prime, I had the following responsibilities:

  • Used UML to model the system behavior with Use Cases, Sequence Diagrams, State Machine Diagrams, Component Diagrams and visually represent relationships between objects with Class Diagrams
  • Design, implementand optimize a matching algorithm for clients and animals using pre-defined attributes and character traits to compute a most suitable set of one to one matches
  • Design a LinkedList with operations specific to the system for easy of non-persistent data manipulation

Automated Smart Vertical Farming Application

Phone App / April 2019 - Present

The project is an ongoing Microsoft hackathon named Discover AI. The challange is to come up with a smart and innovative solution to one or more of the 4 following world problems:

  • Climate Change
  • Quality Education
  • Good Health and Well-Being
  • Sustainable Cities and Communities
I am working with 2 other talented individuals and we decided to design an app that automates your own vertical farming system in your backyard at low costs. The app will prompt the user to enter their dietery needs and then the user will be recommended on what seeds to buy and plant and the app will take care of the rest. The features included are:
  • Suggesting on foods to grow based on dietery needs
  • Updating the user on the time remaining for the foor to grown
  • An image recognition system that gives the user the health content of the product
  • An ecological footprint calculator
